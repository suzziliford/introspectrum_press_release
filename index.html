is a collection of contemplations recorded in the height of the pandemic by Australian Chicago artist Tthew



Conceived as an invitation to enter a space of reflection and curiosity, the album takes the listener progressively deeper into stillness before delivering them to a place of fortified resolve with the chant
Make Trouble Your Friend featuring
Jeremy Cunningham of Resavior



Influenced heavily by ambient music, the album combines the narrative focus of traditional songwriting with experimental elements, highlighting noise and jazz artists from Australia and Chicago



It is the first offering to be released under the moniker Tthew,
